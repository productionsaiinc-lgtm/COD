# Assets

## Art Direction
Modern tactical military aesthetic inspired by Call of Duty Mobile.
Core Visual Pillars: Tactical Realism, High Visibility, Mobile-First Design, Fast Visual Communication.

## Environment
- Ground textures (sand, concrete, grass, asphalt)
- Buildings (apartments, warehouses, military)
- Props (crates, cars, barriers, containers)
- Foliage (trees, bushes)
- Battle Royale: Airplane, supply drops, watchtowers

## Characters & Classes
| Class    | Visual Style          | Features                  |
|----------|-----------------------|---------------------------|
| Medic    | White + green accents | Revive, healing           |
| Ninja    | Dark/stealth          | Silent movement           |
| Defender | Heavy armor           | Extra protection          |

## Weapons
**Categories:** Assault Rifles, SMGs, LMGs, Snipers, Shotguns, Pistols, Launchers

**Attachment Types:** Optics, Muzzle, Barrel, Stock, Underbarrel, Magazine, Laser, Rear Grip

## UI / HUD (Mobile Optimized)
- Large Health + Armor bar
- Big Ammo counter
- Virtual Left Joystick (Movement)
- Virtual Right Joystick (Look)
- Action Buttons: Fire, ADS, Reload, Jump, Sprint, Crouch
- Minimap + Compass + Kill Feed

## Effects & VFX
Muzzle flash, Bullet impacts, Blood, Hit markers, Explosions, Smoke, Safe zone border, Supply drop effects

## Audio (Priority)
Weapon fire (normal + suppressed), Reload, Footsteps, Hit/death sounds, Objective sounds, Safe zone warning, Zombie sounds
